Privacy Policy

How Msg2Git protects your privacy and data

TL;DR: We only collect what's necessary to provide our service. Your GitHub data stays between you and GitHub. We don't sell your data or use it for advertising.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Telegram Account Information: Your Telegram user ID and username when you interact with our bot
  • GitHub Repository Information: Repository URLs and access tokens that you provide to connect your GitHub account
  • Messages and Content: Text messages, photos, and other content you send through the Telegram bot
  • Payment Information: Billing information processed through Stripe for premium features

Information Automatically Collected

  • Usage Data: Statistics about your usage of the service (number of commits, photos uploaded, issues created)
  • Technical Data: Error logs and performance metrics to improve our service
  • Repository Data: File sizes and repository statistics to enforce usage limits

2. How We Use Your Information

Service Provision

  • Process and commit your messages to your GitHub repositories
  • Upload and manage photos in your GitHub repositories
  • Create and manage GitHub issues based on your requests
  • Provide AI-enhanced features like title generation and hashtags

Service Management

  • Enforce usage limits based on your subscription tier
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Monitor service performance and prevent abuse

3. Data Storage and Security

GitHub Data

Your GitHub auth tokens are encrypted and stored securely. We only use these tokens to perform actions you explicitly request through the Telegram bot. Your repository content remains on GitHub and is not stored on our servers.

Message Content

Messages you send are temporarily processed to commit them to your GitHub repository. We do not permanently store your message content on our servers after processing.

Security Measures

  • All sensitive data is encrypted both in transit and at rest
  • GitHub tokens are encrypted using industry-standard encryption
  • Access to user data is strictly limited to necessary service operations
  • Regular security audits and updates

4. Third-Party Services

GitHub

We integrate with GitHub's API to manage your repositories. Your interactions with GitHub are governed by GitHub's Privacy Policy.

Telegram

Our service operates through Telegram's Bot API. Your interactions with Telegram are governed by Telegram's Privacy Policy.

Stripe

Payment processing is handled by Stripe. We do not store your credit card information. Stripe's handling of your payment data is governed by Stripe's Privacy Policy.

AI Providers

When you use AI features, your messages may be processed by third-party AI services. You can configure your own AI API keys to maintain control over this data.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share information
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party services that help us operate our service (GitHub, Telegram, Stripe)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and the safety of our users
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

6. Your Rights and Choices

Access and Control

  • Account Information: You can view and update your account information using bot commands
  • Data Export: Your data is stored in your own GitHub repository, which you control
  • Service Termination: You can stop using our service at any time

Data Deletion

  • You can request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Repository content remains in your GitHub account and is not deleted
  • Some information may be retained for legal or business purposes as required by law

7. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active and for a reasonable period after deactivation
  • Usage Statistics: Aggregated, non-personal usage data may be retained indefinitely for service improvement
  • Payment Data: Billing information is retained as required by law and for tax purposes

8. International Data Transfers

Our service may involve data transfers to countries outside your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data during such transfers, in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of our service after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@msg2git.com
  • Telegram: Through our support bot
Your Privacy Matters: We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices. If you have any concerns or questions, please don't hesitate to reach out.
